Research Interests

Areas I'm passionate about and actively researching

Visual Attention

Predicting where humans look in images and videos using deep learning. Scanpath prediction and saliency estimation.

Medical Imaging

Applying computer vision to medical image analysis, including knee osteoarthritis severity assessment and quality evaluation.

Domain Adaptation

Transferring knowledge across domains, particularly for cultural heritage images and cross-dataset generalization.

Deep Learning

Neural network architectures, self-supervised learning, and representation learning for visual tasks.

LLM Agents

Exploring hallucination mitigation and reasoning capabilities in large language models.

Eye Tracking

Understanding human visual behavior through eye-tracking data analysis and modeling.
