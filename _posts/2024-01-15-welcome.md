Welcome to my new academic website and blog! This site serves as a central hub for my research, publications, and thoughts on computer vision and deep learning.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Research**: Information about my current and past research projects
- **Publications**: A complete list of my peer-reviewed papers
- **Blog**: Thoughts on research, tutorials, and industry insights
- **CV**: My professional experience and qualifications
- **Contact**: Ways to get in touch

## Research Focus

My research interests center around:

1. **Visual Attention Prediction** - Understanding where humans look in images
2. **Scanpath Modeling** - Predicting sequences of eye movements
3. **Medical Imaging** - Applying deep learning to medical image analysis
4. **Domain Adaptation** - Transferring knowledge across different domains

## Recent Work

I recently completed my PhD at the University of Orleans, focusing on modeling human visual attention for cultural heritage images. You can find all my publications on the [Publications](/publications/) page.

Stay tuned for more updates and blog posts about my research!
